What We Do
Rhode Island has assembled 18 employers, including CVS, Citizens Bank, Fidelity, and Innovex, to recruit over 2,000 candidates from fast-track tech training programs that will be developed and expanded over the next five years. The Department of Labor and Training, Commerce RI, and Opportunity@Work are working with Tech Collective, a non-profit workforce intermediary, to strengthen Rhode Island's IT workforce pipeline and train individuals, with a focus on low-income community members.
